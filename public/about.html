India currently has more than 4,450 EdTech solutions that cater to multiple needs across the teaching and learning spectrum. However, there is no clear way to identify which of these solutions are of high quality and will create the most impact on studentsâ€™ learning.
Therefore, the government in partnership with Central Square Foundation has published this easy-to-use database of quality EdTech solutions that have the potential to be deployed at scale. All solutions are free of cost and categorized for use by teachers and students/parents.

Clicking on a card takes you to the website/app download site for the solution.

The aim of this website is to support parents, students and teachers to select the right solutions for their needs and ensure meaningful teacher and learning is being continued at home. However this is not an exhaustive list of quality solutions and we would be happy to review your suggestions.
