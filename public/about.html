Due to the national shutdown of schools to combat the COVID pandemic in India, millions of school students have been forced to discontinue their education. However, with more than % of households having access to either an internet-enabled smartphone or laptop, there is immense potential to use technology to ensure students are learning at home.
In an effort to classify and make easily available EdTech solutions for parents, teachers, and students to ensure continued teaching and learning at home - the government of India in partnership with Central Square Foundation has published this easy-to-use database1 of free EdTech solutions. All solutions listed are free of cost and categorized for use by teachers and students/parents.

If you have suggestions for free EdTech solutions that can be included in this list, please fill this form.
1This is a non-exhaustive database of solutions that have not been rigorously evaluated for efficacy.
